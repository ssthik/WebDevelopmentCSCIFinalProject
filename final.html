A little bit about Cambodia

Cambodia is a country located in the mainland of the Indochinese region, located in Southeast Asia. Cambodia is home to some of the most important world heritage sites throughout the world. Home to the world's largest and oldest religious monument, Angkor Wat , and many other tourists destinations within the region.

Destinations

Cambodia is a land full of stunning temples, world class beaches, and an abundance of many more natural attractions. Still rebuilding from the disaster of the Khmer Rouge Regime there are many parts of the country that is left untouched to maintain its natural environment and beauty.
